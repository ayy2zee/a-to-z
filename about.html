About

A little bit about who I am and what I'm up to.

Hey, I'm Alexy

Welcome to A to Z — a space where I write about the things I'm learning, building, and thinking about. The name is simple: I want to cover it all, from the very beginning of an idea to wherever it ends up.

I started this blog because writing is how I make sense of things. Whether it's a technical problem I solved, a book that changed how I think, or just an observation worth capturing — this is where it lives.
