Hello, World — Why I Started Writing

Every blog starts somewhere. This one starts here, with me trying to explain why I'm doing this at all.

The honest answer: I've been thinking out loud my whole life — in notes, in conversations, in the margins of books — and at some point it made sense to do it somewhere more permanent. Somewhere others could stumble across it if they wanted to.

What "A to Z" means

The name isn't about being comprehensive. It's about being willing to start at the beginning and see where things go. Most good things start messy and incomplete. A to Z is permission to begin — not to have it all figured out.

I'm not committing to a niche. I'm committing to showing up and writing honestly about what's on my mind. Sometimes that'll be technical. Sometimes it'll be reflective. Sometimes it'll be neither.

What you can expect

Posts whenever I have something worth saying — not on a schedule. I'd rather write one good thing a month than seven forgettable ones a week.

If something I write resonates with you, I'd genuinely love to hear about it. There's a contact page for exactly that.

Here we go.
