Hello World

Welcome to A to Z — a digital space where I write about the things I'm learning, building, and thinking about at any given time. The name is simple: I want to cover it all, from the very beginning of an idea to wherever it ends up. And it's also thematically linked to my name and identity.

I started this blog because I realize now that writing is how I make sense of things. Whether it's a technical problem I solved, a book that changed how I think, or just an observation worth capturing — this is where it will live in the online aether.
